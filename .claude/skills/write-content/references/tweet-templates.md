# Viral Tweet Templates

25 proven formats with formulas and examples. Pick the template that matches your content type and adapt.

## 1. Hot take
**Formula:** "[Controversial statement]. Here's why everyone is wrong:"
**Example:** "React is holding the web back. Here's why everyone is wrong about frontend frameworks."
**Engagement type:** Replies (people argue)

## 2. "X things I learned"
**Formula:** "I [did X] for [time period]. Here are [N] things I learned:"
**Example:** "I built 12 SaaS products in 12 months. Here are 9 things I learned that no one talks about:"
**Engagement type:** Bookmarks + retweets

## 3. Thread opener
**Formula:** "[Bold claim or specific result]. [Twist]. Here's the [N]-step breakdown:"
**Example:** "I went from 0 to 10,000 followers in 57 days without posting a single thread. Here's exactly how."
**Engagement type:** Profile clicks + follows

## 4. Personal story
**Formula:** "[Moment in time], [vulnerable situation]. [What changed]."
**Example:** "In 2019, I made a mistake that nearly killed my startup. I had $400 in my bank account. 3 years later, we hit $2M ARR."
**Engagement type:** Likes + replies (emotional connection)

## 5. Prediction
**Formula:** "By [year], [bold prediction]. Most people aren't prepared. Here's why:"
**Example:** "By 2027, 80% of junior dev roles won't exist. Most CS students aren't prepared."
**Engagement type:** Replies (debate) + retweets

## 6. Tutorial/How-to
**Formula:** "How to [achieve result] in [timeframe] (step-by-step):"
**Example:** "How to mass send 1,000 cold emails that actually get replies (step-by-step):"
**Engagement type:** Bookmarks (save for later)

## 7. Before/After
**Formula:** "Before [X]: [bad state]. After [X]: [great state]. The difference? [Key insight]."
**Example:** "Before learning copywriting: 2% conversion. After: 11%. The difference? One framework."
**Engagement type:** Bookmarks + retweets

## 8. Tier list
**Formula:** "[Category] tier list:\nS tier: [items]\nA tier: [items]\nF tier: [items]\nFight me in the replies."
**Engagement type:** Replies (strong opinions)

## 9. Unpopular opinion
**Formula:** "Unpopular opinion: [statement challenging conventional wisdom]."
**Engagement type:** Replies + quote tweets

## 10. "X is overrated"
**Formula:** "[Popular thing] is overrated. [Alternative] is where the real value is."
**Engagement type:** Replies (debate)

## 11. Observation
**Formula:** "I've noticed [pattern]. [Why this matters]."
**Example:** "I've noticed the best founders don't pitch their product. They describe the problem so well you beg for the solution."
**Engagement type:** Likes + retweets (wisdom)

## 12. Question
**Formula:** "[Relatable question your audience is dying to answer]?"
**Example:** "What's one tool you use daily that you think is massively underrated?"
**Engagement type:** Replies (participation)

## 13. Challenge
**Formula:** "I [did X challenge] for [time]. Result: [outcome]. Lesson: [takeaway]."
**Engagement type:** Likes + bookmarks

## 14. "Nobody talks about X"
**Formula:** "Nobody talks about this, but [surprising fact about well-known topic]."
**Engagement type:** Retweets + bookmarks

## 15. List tweet
**Formula:** "[N] [things] you need for [outcome]:\n1. [Item]\n2. [Item]...\nWhich one are you focusing on?"
**Engagement type:** Bookmarks + replies

## 16. Quote tweet commentary
**Formula:** [Quote tweet] + "This. [Strong take]. [Why it matters]."
**Engagement type:** Likes + retweets

## 17. "I spent X hours doing Y"
**Formula:** "I spent [impressive time] [doing Y]. Here's everything I learned (so you don't have to):"
**Engagement type:** Bookmarks + follows

## 18. Comparison
**Formula:** "[Thing A] vs [Thing B]:\n[A]: [traits]\n[B]: [traits]\nChoose wisely."
**Engagement type:** Replies (debate) + bookmarks

## 19. Self-deprecating humor
**Formula:** "[Relatable professional failure told with humor]"
**Example:** "Some days I wonder if I'm managing Q4 or if Q4 is managing me."
**Engagement type:** Likes + retweets (relatability)

## 20. Behind the scenes
**Formula:** "Everyone sees [impressive result]. Nobody sees [raw reality]. Here's the real timeline:"
**Engagement type:** Likes + bookmarks (authenticity)

## 21. Data share
**Formula:** "[Specific metric]. [Context]. Here's the breakdown:"
**Example:** "I gained 12,487 followers in 61 days. Zero paid ads. Here's the exact breakdown:"
**Engagement type:** Bookmarks + follows

## 22. Myth-busting
**Formula:** "What most people believe: [common belief]. The reality: [surprising truth]."
**Engagement type:** Retweets + replies

## 23. Resource dump
**Formula:** "Looking to [goal]? Here are the best tools:\n- @tool1 (purpose)\n- @tool2 (purpose)\nBookmark this"
**Engagement type:** Bookmarks (highest bookmark ratio of any format)

## 24. PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution)
**Formula:** "[Problem]. [Agitate]. [Solution]."
**Example:** "Struggling to grow on X? The algorithm buries you daily. Fix: Post 3 value threads/week."
**Engagement type:** Likes + replies

## 25. Audience call-out
**Formula:** "[Specific audience]: If you're [pain point], [tease solution]."
**Example:** "Startup founders: Burnt out on funding pitches? My rejection-to-$1M template is inside."
**Engagement type:** Profile clicks + follows
